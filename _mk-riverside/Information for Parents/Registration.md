<b>MK Registration Exercise (2027 Intake)</b><br>

Thank you for visiting us at the MOE Kindergarten Open House!

<b>Online registration for 2027 K1 admissions will take place from Monday, 23rd March 2026 (9 a.m.) to Tuesday, 31st March 2026 (4.30 p.m.).</b>

Eligibility:

*   Open to Singapore Citizen and Permanent Resident children born between <b>2nd January 2022 and 1st January 2023 </b>(both dates inclusive).
    

How to Register:

*   Register your child at <a target="blank" href="https://go.gov.sg/mk-register">https://go.gov.sg/mk-register</a> during the registration period.
    

Registration Outcome:

*   You will be notified of the registration outcome via email by <b>1st June 2026.</b>
    

Should you have any questions, please visit <a target="blank" href="www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten">www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten</a> for more information.

We look forward to welcoming your child to MOE Kindergarten!
