<b>MK Registration Exercise (2027 Intake)</b><br>
Thank you for visiting us at the MOE Kindergarten Open House!

*Registration Status*
- Online registration for 2027 K1 admissions (held from 23 to 31 March 2026) has now closed.
- If you missed the registration period, we invite you to join our waitlist at this link: <a target="blank" href="https://go.gov.sg/mkriverside-waitlist">https://go.gov.sg/mkriverside-waitlist</a>.

*Eligibility:*
- Open to Singapore Citizen and Permanent Resident children born between 2nd January 2022 and 1st January 2023 (both dates inclusive).

*Registration Outcome:*
- Applicants who registered during the open registration period (23 March - 31 March) will be notified of the outcome via email by 1st June 2026.

Should you have any questions, please visit <a target="blank" href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten">https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten</a> for more information.

We look forward to welcoming your child to MOE Kindergarten!
