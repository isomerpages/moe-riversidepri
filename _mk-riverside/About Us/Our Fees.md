**MOE Kindergarten Fees**
-------------------------

The monthly fee for MK in 2021 and 2022 is:

*   $160 for Singapore Citizens (SCs).
    
*   $320 for Singapore Permanent Residents (PRs).
    

MK fees are also payable during the June and December holidays.
